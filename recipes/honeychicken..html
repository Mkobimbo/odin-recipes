Honey glazed chicken

How to make honey glazed chicken

This is a quick guide on how to make honey glazed chicken. So you may be butting your head as to what exactly honey glazed chicken is, well the explanation is simple, its just pre_cooked chicken with a good layer of some sweet honey.

Ingredients

  • quarter cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/8 tablespoon of red pepper flakes
  • one and a half tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl, set aside
  3. heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until light brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes or more
  5. Serve and enjoy!
